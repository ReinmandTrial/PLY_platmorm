Choose best companies with our scoring system

PLY scoring is a ranking system that reflects relative investment attractiveness of companies. System is based on five key financial aspects (areas):

Valuation Growth Profitability Financial strenght Free cash flow

Each of these components is also ranked based on most important fundamental metrics. Total Score is calculated as a simple average of these components’ ranks. Total Score ranges from 0 to 10, with 10 as a highest rank that reflects most attractive companies from investing perspective.

Discover undervalued stocks with our relative valuation methodology

PLY provides Fair Value estimations for company’s equity (stock) and compares it to actual Market capitalization. Respectively, Valuation Gap is calculated for every company’s stock as a difference between actual Market Cap and Fair Value. Negative Valuation Gap indicates that company’s stock is undervalued compared to its calculated Fair Value. Positive Valuation Gap indicates that company’s stock is overvalued compared to its calculated Fair Value.
