Discover undervalued stocks with our relative valuation methodology

PLY provides Fair Value estimations for company’s equity (stock) and compares it to actual Market capitalization. Respectively, Valuation Gap is calculated for every company’s stock as a difference between actual Market Cap and Fair Value. Negative Valuation Gap indicates that company’s stock is undervalued compared to its calculated Fair Value. Positive Valuation Gap indicates that company’s stock is overvalued compared to its calculated Fair Value.
